QUALITY POLICY & MISSION VISION | VALUES

QUALITY POLICY

 

We are committed to our patients with focus on quality and cost-effective healthcare services provided by competent and compassionate staff in a safe and healthy environment using latest technology and well-optimized quality management system (based on the standards of ISO 9001).

 

VISION

 

To be distinguished as the healthcare provider of choice where achieving quality of life (QOL) is passion and CARING is service.

 

MISSION

 

SAGHI, at all times, will provide exceptional quality care as a leading hospital in Quezon and nearby provinces with steadfast attention to comprehensive range of services, which are cost effective, delivered by competent team of staff through modern technologies in a safe and sound environment, designed to provide exceptional value to our stakeholders and community.
